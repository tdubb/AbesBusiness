What We Do

ABES has developed and patented an innovative biotechnological solution that significantly increases the production of renewable methane gas from the tried and proven anaerobic digestion process.

The ABES three-stage anaerobic digestion system increases the allowable flow throughput of the biological process, significantly reduces inhibition effects, and isolates and optimizes environmental conditions of the methane producing microbial populations in the final stage of the system. It operates by the addition of two smaller reaction tanks (ABES Stages II and III) connected in series to the original primary anaerobic digester, which functions as Stage I of the ABES system. ABES Stage II, operated as an upflow flocculent sludge bed digester, completes hydrolysis, hydrogen gas production and removal of suspended solids, while ABES Stage III, an upflow packed bed rector using the ABES patent pending biofilm attachment media, is optimized to significantly increase production levels of clean renewable methane gas.
